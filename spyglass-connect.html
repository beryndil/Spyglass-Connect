Setup

Connected in
only seconds

01
Launch Spyglass Connect on your PC
It auto-detects your Minecraft worlds from the default launcher, Prism Launcher, or any custom path you add.
02
Scan the QR code with Spyglass on your phone
One scan establishes a persistent encrypted connection. Keys are saved — you'll never need to scan again.
03
Play. Your phone keeps up.
Every time Minecraft auto-saves, your phone gets the latest data. Inventory, chests, map — all live.
