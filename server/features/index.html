Server Features

Plugins that protect, not plugins that change.

Performance

The server runs on optimized software to keep gameplay smooth. No lag, no excuses. Chunk loading, entity ticking, and redstone all run at full speed without cutting corners.

Security

Anti-cheat protection keeps the playing field level. Account security measures prevent unauthorized access. You play fair, we make sure everyone else does too.

Land Protection

Claim your territory and control who can build, break, or interact within it. Your builds are safe whether you’re online or off. No more logging in to find your house flattened.

Graves

When you die, your items stay safe at your death location in a grave. Here’s the catch — and it’s intentional:

  • No compass pointing to your grave
  • No directional arrows in your HUD
  • No coordinates displayed in chat

You retrace your steps and find it the old-fashioned way. It’s Minecraft, not GPS. Your items are protected, but the adventure of getting them back is yours.

Why? Because handing you a compass and a waypoint removes the tension and exploration that makes death meaningful. The grave protects your items — but you earn them back.

Crossplay

Java and Bedrock players share the same world. No separate servers, no compromises. Play with your friends no matter what platform they’re on — PC, phone, console, or tablet.

We don’t add plugins to change the game — we add them to protect it.
