<details>
<summary>How do I join the server?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
Add <code>mc.hardknocks.university</code> to your server list. Java Edition connects directly; Bedrock Edition uses port <code>19132</code>. Check the full <a href="/server/join/">How to Join</a> guide for step-by-step instructions.
</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What version is the server running?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
The server runs Minecraft <strong>1.21.1</strong> with Java and Bedrock crossplay enabled.
</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I use Optifine or Sodium?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
Yes. Performance mods like Optifine, Sodium, Lithium, and cosmetic shader packs are all allowed. Minimaps are fine as long as they don't show caves or entities. X-ray texture packs and hacked clients are not allowed.
</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do land claims work?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
You can claim areas of land to protect your builds from griefing. Claimed land prevents other players from building, breaking, or interacting within your territory unless you grant them access.
</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What happens when I die?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
A grave spawns at your death location keeping your items safe. You won't get a compass, directional arrow, or coordinates — you'll need to remember where you were and find your way back. Your items are protected; the journey back is part of the game.
</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Will the world ever reset?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
<strong>No.</strong> The world is permanent. Your builds are here to stay. Major Minecraft updates that add new terrain generation will be handled by expanding the world border, not resetting.
</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is there a Discord server?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
Coming soon. Stay tuned.
</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I apply for staff?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
Staff applications will open when the community grows. For now, be active, be helpful, and be a good member of the community — that's the best application.
</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Are there custom items or economy plugins?</summary>
<div class="faq-answer">
No. The server is as close to vanilla as possible. No custom items, no server currency, no teleportation commands. Plugins are only used for protection, security, and quality of life.
</div>
</details>
